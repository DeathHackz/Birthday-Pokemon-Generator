Find My Pokémon!

Features

  • Generate a special pokémon based on your birthday
  • Display official artwork by Ken Sugimori of your pokémon
  • Link to multiple resources for more info about your pokémon
  • Allow you to share your pokémon on various social media sites

About

This is just a simple website to find your special pokémon.

This started out as a project for a coding program, but soon turned into a really awesome time-killer. This website is my first second real attempt at making a website with fully functioning features. This current website was rewritten from the ground up, as an attempt to improve the original, which can be found here. Hopefully you enjoy using my site, and share it with others.

This is still not professionally made by no means, its just a stepping stone in hopes to improve myself, and my skills.
